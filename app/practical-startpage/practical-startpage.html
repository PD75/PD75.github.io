About

Practical Startpage is a simple yet powerful extensions for google chrome, allowing easy access to browser data, such as bookmarks and history.

  • Responsive Design, adjust to all screens
  • Configurable, you can decide what widgets go where
  • Open Source, you can develop your own widgets (GitHub)
  • Bookmark tree with text search and bookmark editor
  • Quicklinks with link from bookmark folder of your choice
  • History, Apps, etc. from chrome
  • RSS Feed for e.g. news
